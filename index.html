What We Do

Over the past 30 years Smallwood Woodworking, owned by Bruce Smallwood, has accumulated the skills to design and built excellent pieces of furniture to meet your unique desires. no matter how unique it is.

We can create anything that your imagination can think of, including:

  • Kitchens
  • Built-ins
  • Tables
  • Vanities
  • And more!

Who We Are

Bruce has been working with wood since he was strong enough to swing a hammer. As a kid he took as many shop class's as he could and worked at a lumber yard as a young adult. He quickly realized that working with wood was more than just a job, it was a passion he could use to bring people joy. Over the last 30 years Bruce has become a master craftsman being able to create amazing and unique pieces.

Where We Are

Our shop is located in Berryville, a small town in historic Shenandoah Valley.
